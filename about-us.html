ABOUT START.NG

All you need to know about Start.ng

WHAT IS START.NG?

Start.ng is a pre-internship training by HNG for complete beginners. They offer the best on hands practice for people in the tech community. They have a set of experienced Developers and facilitators in every aspect of New generation tech, ranging from Frontend, Backend, Mobile and Web app developers, Machine learning, Digital Marketers, software engineers e.t.c.

Start.ng is a platform that gives everyone an equal opportunity of learning in the tech world, in order to develop and build new generation Tech Developers. It is a learning community where mentors use their already acquired skills to help newbies get better. It is the best place to be an aspiring developer and get introduced to better developers and mentors. They support vision to see the tech community grow by providing necessary resources and mentorship to young talents who are passionate about making impact in the tech world. It is indeed a golden opportunity to grow and connect with like minds in the tech and digital space, making the tech ecosystem to keep waxing stronger. Actually, anyone is free to join the pre internship, but only those that are up to par make it through the stages and to the very end. The organizers chip in some set of tasks as an initial filter this is meant to narrow the focus and mentoring to the target audience for the internship.

Benefits of Start.ng Internship

The following are basic tips to help applicants gets started:

  • Interns are to get acquainted with the basic collaboration tools and work environment like slack, github, xamp/wamp, google sheets etc.
  • Interns are to ask questions when in doubt and there are mentors to help clarify any concern.
  • Interns are to conplete given tasks before deadline.
  • Interns are to follow given instructions.
  • Interns are to be team players and always open to ideas and suggestion.

Basic tips for interns

The following are benefits of Start.ng Internship:

  • Interns learn everything they need to know about becoming a tech enthusiast for free.
  • Interns get promoted to the HNG internship after the pre-Internship.
  • Finalists get hired after the HNG internship
  • Projects are often outsourced to interns that complete the program.
  • Interns get paid when they deliver.
